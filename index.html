Welcome To 44 SAO PAULO Lounge

Welcome to SAO PAULO Lounge, where vibrant nights and unforgettable experiences come to life. Established in 2023, we have become a staple in the heart of Abuja, offering a pulsating atmosphere for those seeking the epitome of nightlife excitement.

Enjoy Yourself

  • Live Performances

    Immerse yourself in the pulsating beats and rhythms that fill the air. Our nightclub features a diverse range of music genres, from the latest hits to timeless classics, creating an electric atmosphere that keeps the dance floor alive all night long.

  • VIP Lounge Experience

    Elevate your night to a VIP level of luxury and exclusivity. Our VIP lounge offers a sophisticated retreat with exclusive seating, personalized bottle service, and a more intimate setting.

  • Strippers

    Experience the allure and excitement of live striptease performances that add a touch of seduction to your night. Our talented performers showcase their skills with elegance and style, creating an unforgettable and visually captivating entertainment experience.

  • Interactive Games

    Take entertainment to the next level with our selection of interactive games. Whether you're challenging friends to a game of billiards, testing your skills at arcade classics, or participating in themed gaming nights, there's no shortage of fun and friendly competition.

  • Gourmet Culinary Delights

    Elevate your night out with a culinary journey that transcends the ordinary. Indulge in gourmet delights featuring a fusion of local and international cuisines. From tantalizing appetizers to exquisite main courses, our menu is crafted to satisfy every palate.

  • Drinks

    Raise your glass to an extensive selection of beverages that cater to every taste. Sip on expertly crafted cocktails, enjoy a wide array of spirits and wines, and experience the thrill of our mixologists creating signature drinks that define the essence of our nightlife.
